What is Kittens of Chaos

Kittens of Chaos is a living transmedia mythic universe created by Mömther. You might look at it and think... cat content! And you would not be entirely wrong. But beneath that, you will find a cosmos where cats are multidimensional beings able to walk many paths, realms shift and breathe between dimensions, and chaos itself has a language all its own.


The main story centers around the Chaos Realm; a symbolic system that contains Eight Houses, each one representing a fundamental metaphysical force. And at the very center, outside and above all of the Houses, you will find Mömther... the Octarine Axis. She is not a House. She is the heart that connects them all.

"There is a Realm that remembers you. Cats walk its edges. Chaos guards its gate."

The Living Storyworld

Now, you might ask... what is a living storyworld, exactly? It is not just a fictional setting! It is an evolving mythic framework that is designed to feel partially alive; as though the Realm is unfolding all around you while you stand inside it.


Think of it this way. The storyworld is the stage, the places and the characters and the lore. But the living mythos is the heartbeat beneath all of that; the ancient archetypes and symbols that are still being discovered even now. Together, they make KoC not just something to watch, but something to live inside.

Active Threats

The MachineRealm. The Ruinborne Corruption. It is an existential, machine-like threat that spreads through realms and warps them from the inside out. Every thousand years or so, its influence rises to a peak... and that time, I am afraid, is very much now.
