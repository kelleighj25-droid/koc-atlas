What is Kittens of Chaos

Kittens of Chaos is a living transmedia mythic universe created by Kelleigh "Mömther" Jones-Coles. On the surface, it looks like playful cat content — but beneath that is a cosmos where cats are fairy creatures, realms shift between dimensions, and chaos itself has a language.


The universe is organized around the Chaos Star — an eight-pointed symbolic system housing Eight Houses, each representing a fundamental metaphysical force. At the center, outside and above all Houses, stands Mömther — the Octarine Axis. Not a House. The heart that connects them all.

"There is a Realm that remembers you. Cats walk its edges. Chaos guards its gate."
Liminality Sacred Chaos Dimensional Travel Paradox Magic Memory & Myth Transformation Cosmic Horror Dark Whimsy
The Living Storyworld

KoC is not just a fictional setting. It is an evolving mythic framework designed to feel partially alive — as though the Realm is unfolding while the audience stands inside it.


The storyworld is the stage — places, characters, and lore. The living mythos is the heartbeat beneath it — ancient archetypes and symbols still being discovered. Together they make KoC not just entertaining, but meaningful. A world you can visit and a myth you can live inside.

Active Threats

The MachineRealm — The Ruinborne Corruption. An existential machine-like threat spreading through realms, warping them from the inside. Every thousand years, its influence peaks. That time is now.

The Little Season has begun. Houses mobilize. Portals open. Sable has already gone through.
